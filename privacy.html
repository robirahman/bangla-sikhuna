Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

বাংলা শিক্ষা (Learn Bengali) is a language-learning app that runs entirely in your browser. Your privacy is simple: we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data storage

All app data — your profiles, learning progress, quiz scores, and settings — is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

Data collection

This app does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies (other than what your browser uses for local storage)
  • No third-party analytics or advertising services
  • No account creation or authentication with external services

Third-party services

The app loads the Noto Sans Bengali font from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy applies to that request. No other third-party services are used.

Children's privacy

Because no data is collected from any user, no data is collected from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer at robirahman.com.
